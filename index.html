Our Journey

Five years of love, laughter, and unforgettable memories

Our story began at System Plus College Foundation in November 2013, where our paths crossed and our hearts connected in an instant. After months of laughter, shared dreams, and countless conversations, she said "Yes" on February 11, 2014, marking the official beginning of our journey as a couple. For five beautiful years, from 2014 to 2019, we embraced each moment with love and dedication, supporting each other through life's ups and downs, and cherishing every memory we created together. On April 27, 2019, surrounded by our loved ones, we exchanged vows and pledged our love to each other in a heartfelt ceremony, sealing our commitment to a lifetime of happiness and adventure. And now, as we celebrate our 5th anniversary, we look back with gratitude for the love we've shared, the obstacles we've overcome, and the growth we've experienced together. Here's to many more years of love, laughter, and unforgettable moments as we continue our journey side by side. Happy 5th Anniversary to us!
